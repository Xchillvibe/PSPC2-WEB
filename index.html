About the Game

Storyline

*Echoes of Friendship* is an emotionally immersive narrative-driven adventure game set against the backdrop of the Ukrainian refugee crisis in Belgium during 2023-2024. You play as Alexander, a young student who finds himself drawn to a mysterious Ukrainian refugee named Platon amidst the chaos and uncertainty of war and displacement.

Gameplay

Navigate through heartfelt chapters, overcoming language barriers, cultural differences, and personal discovery. Forge a meaningful connection with Platon and other new students in your community.
