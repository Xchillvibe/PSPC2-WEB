About the Game

Echoes of Friendship is an emotionally immersive narrative-driven adventure game set against the backdrop of the Ukrainian refugee crisis in Belgium during 2023-2024. You play as Alexander, a young student who finds himself drawn to a mysterious Ukrainian refugee named Platon amidst the chaos and uncertainty of war and displacement. Navigate language barriers, cultural differences, and LGBTQ themes to forge a meaningful connection with Platon and the new students in your community.
